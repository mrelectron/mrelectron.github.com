Strategy Alexandra

Apr 29th, 2012

The Alexandra strategy will be one of the strategies utilised by a trading group I have joined. This group will trade the London open from 0800-1100 UTC each day.

I have not backtested Alexandra at this point.

You can ignore the following text in brackets [has been backtested on the following instruments [AUDUSD, EURUSD …] and in the following timeframes [M5, M15, H1 …]. Backtest documentation and results are located [here]].

Money Management

  • Risk Percent: 1.5% of account equity.
  • Leverage: 50:1
  • PositionSize: 0.3 Lots.

Chart Configuration

Alexandra utilises multi-timeframe analysis. Four charts(M15, H1, H4, D1) are required. Each chart will have an ADX and 4 EMA indicators of differing lags. The configuration of the ADX, EMA’s can be found in the book.

Entry:Long

use pseudo code Initiate LONG position when the RSI (7) touches the 25 level from below, wait for the first Blue MACD dot to form and then go LONG.

Stop

use pseudo code. employ the use of Trailing Stops or place your stop loss below the MACD dots.

Exit

use pseudo code. you can exit your open position when a red MACD DOT forms above the candlestick or you can wait for the RSI to touch the 75 level from below.
